# Free Courses

### 1. Web3 University

A comprehensive educational resource offering tutorials across the Web3 ecosystem, including tracks like "The Road to Web3" and "Create Your First Smart Contract." 

**Visit:** https://www.web3.university

### 2. CryptoZombies

An interactive platform that teaches you Solidity by guiding you through building your own crypto-collectibles game.

**Visit:** https://cryptozombies.io

### 3. Web3 Foundation's "Blockchain for Beginners" MOOC

This course teaches blockchain from the basics up to building your own blockchain.

**Visit:** https://www.theinsaneapp.com/2022/03/best-web3-courses.html
